Despicable Me is an American computer-animated media franchise created by Sergio Pablos. It is centered on a former supervillain turned secret agent named Gru and his yellow-colored Minions.The franchise is produced by Illumination and distributed by its parent company Universal Pictures.
Episode-2

Gru is recruited by agent Lucy Wilde, who is investigating a theft of the mutagen called PX-41, stolen from the Arctic Circle. During their investigations, they extract a supervillain with the goal of causing world domination after faking his death.
Episode-3

Gru teams up with his long-lost twin brother Dru to stop Balthazar Bratt, a former child actor of the 1980s, from destroying Hollywood, Los Angeles, after his show is canceled.

A short film titled Mower Minions was released in 2016 with The Secret Life of Pets, being the first short film to be released theatrically. A second theatrical short film was released in 2018 with The Grinch, while another one was released in The Grinch's DVD and Blu-ray.
